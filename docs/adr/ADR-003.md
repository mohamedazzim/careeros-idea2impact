# Architecture Decision Record: ADR-003 — Why NV-Embed-v1 as the Dense Representation Model

## Status
Approved

## Context
In CareerOS AI, a user's resume, job experience, and goals must be matched to high-dimensional job descriptions. Standard embeddings models often fail to capture complex career relationships, resulting in high misclassification rates.

## Decision
NV-Embed-v1 was selected as our core dense embeddings representation model because of its architectural superiority:

1. **Top-Tier MTEB Benchmark Performance**: NV-Embed-v1 ranks at the absolute pinnacle of the Massive Text Embedding Benchmark (MTEB), specifically demonstrating superior performance across text retrieval, ranking, and semantic classification parameters.
2. **Instruction-Tuned Dense Mappings**: Supports custom instructional prefixes (e.g., *"Instruct: Retrieve relevant technical roles based on candidate experiences; Query: ..."*), letting the model dynamically align search priorities depending on the query context.
3. **Bidirectional Attention with High Tokens Window**: Provides unmatched attention context capturing complete long-form professional experience histories without truncation.
4. **Optimized Cosine Similarity Distribution**: Emits dense vector representations that distribute uniformly across high-octant mathematical hyperspaces, maximizing separation margins between distinct professional fields.

## Consequences
- **Positive**: Pristine RAG retrieval precision, high semantic alignment, and instruction-aware multi-modal classification matches.
- **Negative**: Massive dense dimensions (4096 dimensions) require increased storage footprints in Qdrant and high GPU memory capacities during deployment.
